5. **Delisting causes**: (a) www vs non-www split — 500 pages indexed under both, no canonical/301 redirect; (b) Slug typos creating duplicate case URLs; (c) Test case `/cases/69834220/test-case/overview` indexed; (d) CTR collapse on high-impression pages.
6. **18 cannibalization issues**: 12 of 18 are URL architecture bug (duplicate Arizona court paths) — fix is 301 redirects. 6 genuine cannibalization issues remain.
7. **Attorney keywords**: "Lis pendens" at Position 1, 91 impressions = attorney-intent. Software/workflow-intent keywords absent.
8. **92% dashboard engagement**: Only 30 users (likely team/beta) — not reliable satisfaction proxy.
9. **juristai.org referrals**: 77.2% engagement = confirmed sister product cross-traffic, best quality acquisition channel.
10. **"alien enemies act judge ruling"**: Position 1, low CTR — pro se users searching current legal events landing on LitigAI. Asset, not noise. Title/meta needs rewrite.
