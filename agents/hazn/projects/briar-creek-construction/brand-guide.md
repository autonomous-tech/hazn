# Brand Guide — Briar Creek Construction

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Briar Creek Construction  
**Tagline:** *Built Right. Built to Last.*  
**Personality:** Dependable, hardworking, rooted in the community. No fluff — just solid craftsmanship.

---

## Logo Concept

- Wordmark-first approach
- Icon: A simple angular roofline or creek/beam mark (clean, geometric)
- Style: Bold, grounded — not trendy

---

## Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex |
|------|------|-----|
| Primary | Deep Forest Green | `#2D4A2D` |
| Secondary | Warm Slate | `#4A5568` |
| Accent | Ohio Amber | `#D4870A` |
| Light BG | Off-White | `#F7F5F0` |
| Dark BG | Almost Black | `#1A1A1A` |
| Text | Charcoal | `#2D2D2D` |
| Border | Light Gray | `#E2E0DB` |

**Rationale:** Green signals growth + outdoor Ohio. Amber pops for CTAs (phone button). Slate is professional. Off-white feels like premium print materials vs sterile tech white.

---

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| Display / H1 | **Oswald** | 700 | Strong, condensed — classic American blue-collar authority |
| Headings H2–H4 | **Oswald** | 500 | Consistent family |
| Body | **Inter** | 400/500 | Clean, readable at all sizes |
| Accent / Labels | **Inter** | 600, uppercase, tracked | For tags, badges, section labels |

Google Fonts: `Oswald:wght@500;700` + `Inter:wght@400;500;600`

---

## Imagery Style

- **Real construction sites** — poured foundations, framed houses, finished homes
- **Ohio landscapes** — rolling hills, neighborhoods, seasons
- **Workers on site** — showing real crew, real work (not stock-model smiles)
- **Before/After** — transformation shots build trust
- **AI generation notes (nano-banana-pro):** Prompt style: *"Residential construction site in rural Ohio, overcast sky, workers framing a house, photorealistic, cinematic, Canon 5D"*

---

## UI Components

### Phone CTA Button
- Background: `#D4870A` (Amber)
- Text: White, bold, uppercase
- Icon: Phone icon left-aligned
- Size: Large — dominant on mobile
- Text: `CALL NOW: (555) 000-0000`

### Section Labels
- Uppercase, tracked, small — `#D4870A` amber
- e.g. `OUR SERVICES`, `WHY BRIAR CREEK`, `GET STARTED`

### Cards / Service Tiles
- White background
- Subtle border `#E2E0DB`
- Green accent line on hover
- Bold Oswald heading

---

## Don'ts

- No tech startup colors (bright blue/purple)
- No thin/light fonts
- No corporate stock photography (boardrooms, handshakes)
- No jargon ("synergize," "solutions," "leverage")
- No cluttered layouts — big images, breathing room

---

## Voice & Tone

- **Direct:** "We build it right the first time."
- **Local:** "Serving Ohio homeowners since [year]"
- **Confident:** Not boastful — earned confidence
- **Warm but brief:** Short sentences. No filler.
