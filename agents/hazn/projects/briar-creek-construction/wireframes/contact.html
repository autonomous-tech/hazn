Common Questions

Answers to what homeowners usually ask before getting started.

How long does the estimate process take? +
Once you submit a request or call, we'll schedule a site visit within a few business days. After seeing the project in person, we typically provide your written estimate within 3–5 business days.
Do you handle all permits and inspections? +
Yes — we handle all required permits, inspections, and coordination with your local municipality. You don't have to navigate the permit process on your own.
What areas do you serve? +
We primarily serve the greater Columbus area — Franklin, Delaware, Licking, Fairfield, Pickaway, Union, Madison, and Knox counties. Not sure if we cover your area? Give us a call and we'll let you know.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Absolutely. We hold a full Ohio General Contractor license and carry comprehensive liability and workers' compensation insurance. We're happy to provide documentation upon request.
How do you handle project communication? +
You'll have a dedicated point of contact throughout your project. We provide regular milestone updates, are reachable by phone and text, and schedule regular walkthroughs so you're never left in the dark.
Do you offer a warranty on your work? +
Yes. All of our projects come with a workmanship warranty. Specific terms vary by project scope — ask about warranty details during your estimate.
