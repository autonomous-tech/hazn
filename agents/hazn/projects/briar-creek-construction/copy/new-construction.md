## OUR PROCESS SECTION

**Section Label:** How a New Build Works With Us

**Headline:** From Empty Lot to Front Door — Here's What to Expect

**Step 1 — Site Assessment & Estimate**
We walk the lot, review your plans or vision, and give you a written estimate. No vague numbers.

**Step 2 — Permits & Pre-Construction**
We handle permit applications and coordinate engineering as needed. You stay informed without drowning in paperwork.

**Step 3 — Site Prep & Foundation**
Clearing, grading, excavation, and foundation — done right before we frame a single wall.

**Step 4 — Framing & Mechanicals**
Our crew frames the structure. Licensed subcontractors handle plumbing, electrical, and HVAC — coordinated by us, not left to chance.

**Step 5 — Insulation, Drywall & Interior Finishes**
This is where your home starts to look like your home. We stay on schedule and keep you in the loop on finish selections.

**Step 6 — Exterior, Roofing & Windows**
We close up the structure and make sure the envelope is tight. Ohio winters don't forgive sloppy work here.

**Step 7 — Final Inspections & Walkthrough**
We walk the finished home with you before you ever sign off. If something isn't right, we fix it — before it's your problem.
