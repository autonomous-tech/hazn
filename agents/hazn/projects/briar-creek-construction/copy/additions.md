## HOW IT WORKS SECTION

**Section Label:** Our Addition Process

**Headline:** Here's How We Build Your Addition

**Step 1 — Free On-Site Estimate**
We walk your property, review your ideas, and assess your existing structure. We give you a written estimate — no vague ranges.

**Step 2 — Design Coordination**
We work with you (and your architect if you have one) to finalize plans that integrate properly with your existing home. [We can connect you with a trusted architect if needed.]

**Step 3 — Permits**
We handle permit applications with your local municipality. No surprises with inspections.

**Step 4 — Site Prep & Foundation**
For room additions and second stories, we start with the right foundation. For garage builds, we prepare the slab or crawl space correctly.

**Step 5 — Framing & Mechanicals**
We frame the addition and coordinate plumbing, electrical, and HVAC as needed for the new space.

**Step 6 — Exterior Match**
We match your roofline, siding, windows, and trim to your existing home. The goal: it looks like it was always there.

**Step 7 — Interior Finish**
Insulation, drywall, flooring, trim, and paint — finished to match or complement your existing interior.

**Step 8 — Final Walkthrough**
We walk it with you before we consider the project complete.
