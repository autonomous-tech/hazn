Membership in the IDU

Membership in the IDU is open to centre-right democratic parties that meet the organisation's membership criteria. Member parties share a commitment to democracy, freedom, and the rule of law.

Political parties wishing to enquire about membership are encouraged to contact the Secretariat directly for information on the application process.
