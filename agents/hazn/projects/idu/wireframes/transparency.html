Funding

Financial Transparency

The International Democracy Union is funded primarily through membership dues contributed by its member parties. The IDU does not receive commercial funding, advertising revenue, or contributions from governments or state entities.

This funding model ensures the IDU's independence and its ability to represent the interests of its member parties without external influence.

Funding Structure

✓ Membership dues from member parties
✓ No commercial funding or advertising
✓ No government or state contributions
✓ Independent of external financial influence
