SECRETARY GENERAL PORTRAIT

Rt. Hon. Stephen Harper

Chairman & Secretary General · IDU
Conservative Party of Canada

The Rt. Hon. Stephen Harper served as Prime Minister of Canada from 2006 to 2015. Under his leadership, Canada navigated the global financial crisis with distinction, emerging as one of the world's strongest economies. He championed free trade, fiscal responsibility, and Canada's role on the world stage.

As Chairman and Secretary General of the IDU since 2018, Mr. Harper has led the organisation through a period of democratic challenges globally, expanding IDU membership to over 80 parties and launching major new initiatives in democracy promotion and party-building support.

He is author of Right Here, Right Now (2018), a comprehensive study of conservative populism and democratic governance in the twenty-first century.
