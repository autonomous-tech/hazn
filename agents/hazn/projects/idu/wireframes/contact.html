Membership

Interested in IDU Membership?

Membership in the IDU is open to centre-right democratic parties that meet the organisation's membership criteria.

Political parties that share the IDU's values of democracy, freedom, and the rule of law may apply for membership. Enquiries are welcome from parties worldwide that meet the membership criteria.
