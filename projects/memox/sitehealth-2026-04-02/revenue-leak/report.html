Critical

Zero attribution infrastructure Observed

With no analytics tag installed, there is no attribution data at all. No source/medium tracking, no campaign parameters, no referral data. If Memox runs outbound campaigns, social posts, or paid ads, there is no way to know which efforts generate pipeline.

Medium Priority

No UTM architecture established Assessment

No evidence of UTM parameter usage on any external links or campaigns. Before launching any paid or organic campaigns, Memox needs a UTM naming convention. Recommended format:
