Cross-Audit Insights

Three themes run across all four audits

1. Invisible to everything that matters

GA4 is not installed (Revenue Leak 18/100). Blog content is blocked from crawlers (SiteScore). No off-site entity presence (SiteScore). No conversion tracking (Revenue Leak). Great content exists but can't be found, and when visitors arrive, their journey is untracked. Root cause: missing analytics infrastructure gates all other progress.

2. The proof gap

Only 1 customer testimonial (ConversionIQ). No G2, Crunchbase, or third-party reviews (SiteScore). No trust badges (UX/UI). No independent press coverage (SiteScore). For a product selling at $349-$799/mo to cautious equipment dealers, the proof layer must match the messaging quality.

3. Binary conversion path

The only way to convert is "Book a Demo" via Calendly (ConversionIQ + UX/UI). No secondary CTA, no email capture, no downloadable content. Phone number exists but is untracked (Revenue Leak). This creates a high-commitment, low-capture funnel that loses potential leads at every stage.
