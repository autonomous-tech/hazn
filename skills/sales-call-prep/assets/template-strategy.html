Objection Handling

"We already have a dev team that does this"
"That's exactly why this works. Your team is great at building websites and apps — that's high-value work. We take the martech plumbing off their plate so they can focus on what generates the most revenue. Think of us as an extension of your team for a very specific skill set, not a replacement."
"We don't outsource our dev work"
"I get that. But this isn't outsourcing your dev work — it's outsourcing a specialty. You wouldn't ask your web devs to do your tax accounting. Server-side tracking, CAPI integrations, and CDP implementations are a specialized skill set that most agency dev teams don't touch daily. We do it all day, every day."
"Our clients are sensitive about third parties"
"Totally understand — especially in healthcare and gaming with compliance requirements. That's why we work 100% white-label. We operate under your brand, your project management, your client relationships. The client never knows we exist. We're ghosts with GTM access."
"We use freelancers when we need extra help"
"How's that working? Are they reliable? Do they deliver in 72 hours? Do they have a proven process? Our value isn't just doing the work — it's doing it predictably, quickly, and without you having to vet a new contractor for each project."
"This isn't a priority right now"
"Fair enough. Can I just run one free MarTech Stack Blueprint on one of your casino or healthcare clients? If we don't find anything meaningful, we'll leave you alone. If we do, you'll have ammunition for a client upsell. Either way, you win."
