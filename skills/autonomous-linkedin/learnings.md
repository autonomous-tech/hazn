### 2026-02-25 — Haaris — Positioning Refinement

**Context:** Follow-up conversation refining what Autonomous actually does and how to talk about it.

**Learnings:**
1. Broader than just martech — covers: martech, analytics, Shopify, e-commerce automation
2. Target is still ad/marketing agencies
3. "AI agents" isn't the way in — it's the destination
4. Service ladder:
   - **Audits** (entry) → CRO, martech, analytics audits
   - **Setup** (project) → martech stack, attribution, Shopify fixes
   - **Automation** (unlock) → AI agents that run the stack
   - **Managed** (retainer) → ongoing maintenance and optimization
5. The way in is the PROBLEM, not the solution: "Your attribution is broken" / "Your store is leaking conversions" / "You spend 10 hours on reporting"
6. The solution (agents, setup, automation) follows the problem

**Updated positioning:**
> "We solve the tech problems agencies can't hire for — martech, analytics, Shopify, automation. Sometimes that's setup. Sometimes that's an AI agent. Depends on the problem."

**Action:** Updated learnings. Skill should lead with problems, not solutions.
