# Store Build Module

## Service: Ecommerce Store Development

Build complete Shopify or WooCommerce stores from scratch — designed to convert.

---

## What We Deliver

- **Complete store setup** — Theme, products, collections, navigation
- **Premium theme customization** — Not template trash, custom to your brand
- **Mobile-first design** — 60%+ of traffic is mobile
- **Payment & shipping** — All integrations configured
- **SEO foundations** — Meta tags, structure, speed optimization
- **Launch-ready** — QA tested, documented handoff

## Our Process

### 1. Discovery (Day 1-2)
- Brand review & competitor analysis
- Sitemap & navigation planning
- Feature requirements gathering

### 2. Design & Build (Day 3-10)
- Theme selection & customization
- Product catalog setup
- Homepage & key pages
- Mobile optimization

### 3. Configure (Day 11-14)
- Payment gateways
- Shipping rules
- Tax settings
- Essential apps

### 4. Launch (Day 15-17)
- QA & testing
- Speed optimization
- DNS & go-live
- Handoff documentation
