## Weekly Tasks (Sundays)
- **Memory Reflection** (also has cron job): If it's Sunday and you haven't done this week's reflection:
  1. Review memory/*.md files from the past week
  2. Extract key decisions, preferences, facts, and lessons
  3. Update MEMORY.md with important long-term learnings
  4. Update bank/world.md with new objective facts
  5. Update bank/opinions.md with preference changes (confidence + evidence)
  6. Create/update bank/entities/*.md for people or projects mentioned
  7. Track completion in memory/heartbeat-state.json
