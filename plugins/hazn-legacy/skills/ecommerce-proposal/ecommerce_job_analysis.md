## Strategic Insights

### 1. Service Positioning

Based on 7,695 jobs, Autonomous should position around:

| Service | Market Size | Competition | Our Play |
|---------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| **Performance Optimization** | 25% of jobs | Medium | Quick wins, clear ROI |
| **Store Builds** | 18% of jobs | High | Premium positioning |
| **Ongoing Maintenance** | 30% of jobs | Low | Retainer revenue |
| **CRO/Conversion** | 10% of jobs | Medium | High-value consulting |
| **Integrations** | 17% of jobs | Medium-High | Technical expertise |
| **Migration** | 5% of jobs | Low | Package deal |

### 2. Proposal Must-Haves

Based on what clients explicitly ask for:
1. **Show platform-specific experience** (40%)
2. **Include portfolio links** (23%)
3. **Quote a price** (34%)
4. **Mention timeline** (13%)
5. **Demonstrate communication skills** (30%)

### 3. Price Strategy

- **Avoid the $0-50 tier** — race to bottom
- **Target $100-500 range** — 32% of market, less competition
- **Premium tier ($500-2500)** — 13% of market, serious clients

### 4. Recurring Revenue Opportunity

30% of jobs are ongoing/maintenance — this is where agencies make money:
- Monthly retainers
- Performance monitoring
- Regular updates
- Priority support
