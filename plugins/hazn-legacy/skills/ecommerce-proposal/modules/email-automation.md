Automated revenue on autopilot. Welcome flows, abandoned carts, post-purchase — emails that sell while you sleep.

- **Email platform setup** — Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or Shopify Email

---

## Core Flows We Build

### 1. Welcome Series (3-5 emails)
- Immediate welcome + discount (if applicable)
- Brand story / about us
- Product education
- Social proof / reviews
- Soft sell / bestsellers

### 2. Abandoned Cart (3-4 emails)
- Quick reminder (1 hour)
- Benefit reminder (24 hours)
- Urgency/scarcity (48 hours)
- Final offer (72 hours)

### 3. Browse Abandonment (2-3 emails)
- "Still looking?" reminder
- Related products
- Social proof for viewed items

### 4. Post-Purchase (4-5 emails)
- Order confirmation + brand touch
- Shipping notification
- Delivery + usage tips
- Review request
- Cross-sell / replenishment

### 5. Win-Back (2-3 emails)
- "We miss you" (30-60 days)
- Special offer (90 days)
- Last chance (120+ days)
