# Market-Specific CRO Best Practices

## Pakistan / South Asia

### Payment & Trust
| Element | Best Practice | Impact |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| COD | Badge prominently on PDP + cart | 40-60% of orders are COD |
| EMI | Show monthly payment options | Increases conversion for >PKR 5000 items |
| Trust badges | COD Available, Secure Checkout, Easy Returns | Critical for first-time buyers |
| WhatsApp | Floating button for support | Expected, not optional |

### Shipping
| Element | Best Practice | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| Free threshold | PKR 3000-5000 standard | Show progress bar |
| Delivery time | 3-5 days realistic | Same-day in major cities premium |
| Tracking | WhatsApp updates preferred | SMS also works |

### UX Considerations
- **Mobile-first**: 80%+ traffic is mobile
- **Image compression**: Slow connections common outside cities
- **Local testimonials**: Show Pakistani customers
- **Price anchoring**: Show compare-at prices prominently
- **Urgency**: "Limited stock" messaging effective

### Payment Methods
- COD (dominant)
- Bank transfer
- JazzCash / EasyPaisa (mobile wallets)
- Credit/debit cards (growing)
- BNPL emerging (QisstPay, PostPay)

## UAE

### Payment & Trust
| Element | Best Practice | Impact |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| COD | Still 30-40% of orders | Less than Pakistan but significant |
| Tabby/Tamara | BNPL very popular | +20-30% conversion |
| Apple Pay | Expected for premium brands | Dominant in iOS demographic |
| Trust | "UAE Based" or "Ships from Dubai" | Local presence matters |

### Shipping
| Element | Best Practice | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| Same-day | Expected in Dubai/Abu Dhabi | Premium positioning |
| Free threshold | AED 200-300 standard | Competitive market |
| Returns | Free returns expected | 14-30 day policy |

### UX Considerations
- **Bilingual**: English primary, Arabic option
- **Luxury aesthetic**: Premium design converts better
- **Fast delivery messaging**: Lead with speed
- **Instagram proof**: Heavy influencer culture

### Payment Methods
- Credit/debit cards
- Apple Pay / Google Pay
- Tabby / Tamara (BNPL)
- COD (declining but still used)

## Saudi Arabia (KSA)

### Payment & Trust
| Element | Best Practice | Impact |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| Mada | Local debit card - MUST support | 60%+ of card payments |
| Tabby/Tamara | BNPL dominant | Critical for fashion/electronics |
| COD | 30-50% depending on category | More common outside Riyadh |
| STC Pay | Mobile wallet growing | Young demographic |

### Shipping
| Element | Best Practice | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| Next-day | Expected in major cities | Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam |
| Free threshold | SAR 200-300 | Market expectation |
| Aramex/SMSA | Trusted carriers | Display logos |

### UX Considerations
- **Arabic RTL**: Full RTL support critical
- **Modest imagery**: Culturally appropriate photography
- **Ramadan/Eid**: Major shopping seasons
- **Mobile-dominant**: 90%+ smartphone penetration

### Payment Methods
- Mada (critical)
- Credit cards
- Tabby / Tamara
- STC Pay
- COD

## United States

### Payment & Trust
| Element | Best Practice | Impact |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| Shop Pay | +50% vs guest checkout | Shopify native advantage |
| Apple Pay | Expected on mobile | One-click checkout |
| Klarna/Affirm | BNPL popular for >$100 | Especially fashion |
| PayPal | Trust signal for older demos | Still widely used |

### Shipping
| Element | Best Practice | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-------|

### UX Considerations
- **Fast load times**: Patience very low
- **Reviews critical**: Verified purchase badges
- **Sustainability**: Eco-friendly messaging resonates
- **Accessibility**: ADA compliance expected

- Shop Pay / Apple Pay / Google Pay

## Localization Checklist

### Language & Content
- [ ] Translated product descriptions
- [ ] Local customer reviews/testimonials
- [ ] Culturally appropriate imagery
- [ ] Local sizing (UK vs US sizes)
- [ ] RTL support for Arabic

### Pricing & Currency
- [ ] Local currency display
- [ ] Prices inclusive of VAT/taxes
- [ ] Local payment methods prominent
- [ ] Market-appropriate free shipping threshold

### Shipping & Fulfillment
- [ ] Realistic delivery estimates
- [ ] Local carrier logos
- [ ] Returns policy for market
- [ ] Local customer service hours

### Trust & Social Proof
- [ ] Local trust badges
- [ ] Market-specific payment logos
- [ ] Local business presence (if applicable)
- [ ] Local influencer/press mentions
