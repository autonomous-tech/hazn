# Copy Guide — You-View SoW Writing

## The Rule
Every sentence is written from the client's perspective. They are the subject. They are receiving, owning, gaining.

## Page-by-Page Copy Patterns

### Cover (P1)
- Headline: Name the engagement, not Autonomous. "Your Analytics Implementation" not "Analytics Implementation by Autonomous"
- Price box label: "Your Investment" not "Total" or "Price"
- Meta row order: **Prepared for** (client) → Prepared by (Autonomous) → Date → Valid for

### Scope (P2)
- Section label: "Your Engagement"
- Section title: "What's included in your [X]" or "What you're getting"
- Overview paragraph: Opens with the client's problem/goal, ends with what they'll have. Never starts with "We".
- Scope item titles: Noun phrases describing what the client gets. "Your GTM Container" not "GTM Setup"
- Scope item descriptions: Start with "Your [thing] will..." or "[Thing] gives you..." Never "We will..."

### Deliverables (P3)
- Section label: "What You'll Receive"
- Section title: "Your deliverables"
- Card titles: What the client owns at the end. "Your GTM Container (Published)" not "GTM Setup"
- Card descriptions: "You'll have..." / "Yours to keep..." / "Handed off with full access."
- Timeline label: "Your Timeline"
- Phase descriptions: "By end of Phase 1, you'll have..." not "We complete..."

### Investment (P4)
- Section label: "Your Investment"
- Table header: "What you're getting" not "Item" or "Service"
- Total row: "Your Total" not "Total"
- Payment bar: Left segment = deposit (dark/active), right = final payment (light)
- Terms tone: Plain language, no legalese. Reassuring not defensive.
- Signature label: Client party listed first on the left, Autonomous on the right.

## Scope Item Formula
```
Title: [Short noun phrase — what client gets]
Desc:  [What it includes] + [Why it matters to them] + [What they can do with it]
```

## Example Rewrites

| Draft | You-view |
|---|---|
| We will set up GTM | Your GTM container is configured from scratch — clean architecture, ready to scale |
| We deliver 3 funnels | You'll have 3 conversion funnels live and documented in PostHog |
| Our team migrates GA4 | Your GA4 data moves cleanly into GTM — no gaps, no double-counting |
| We provide documentation | You keep a full reference doc — no black boxes, no dependency on us |

## Tone
- Confident, direct, warm
- Short sentences over long ones
- No filler phrases ("leveraging", "synergies", "best-in-class")
- Numbers over vague claims ("3 funnels" not "multiple funnels")
- If something is included, say "Included" — don't price things at $0
