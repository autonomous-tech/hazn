Autonomous Silver Mirror // How we solve it
Attribution & Tracking
High-intent engagement tracking
We define real intent signals with your team and capture them through a CDP — filtering out bots and scrapers, measuring what actually predicts a booking.
UTMs persisting into the booking engine
PostHog persists UTMs natively across the session with a server-side cookie fallback — source attribution never drops at the booking handoff.
Cross-domain session continuity
GTM configured for wildcard domains plus PostHog session profiling — one unbroken user journey from silvermirror.com through book.silvermirror.com to confirmation.
Accurate ROAS + channel contribution
Server-side tracking paired with Conversions API gives you clean attribution across paid, organic, direct, and referral — no fragile front-end tags, no self-referral inflation.
Our position on the "Safe Fix" approach: front-end GA4 patches are fragile by design. Text-highlight events and lookup tables don't solve the underlying data continuity problem. The right fix is server-side infrastructure — built once, reliable permanently.
AI Traffic & Entity Clarity
AI-assistant impact on bookings
Once server-side is live, your analytics stack natively reports AI-sourced bookings and revenue — no manual lookup tables, no forced parameters, no fragile workarounds.
Detect ChatGPT / Gemini / Perplexity / Copilot
We capture UTMs natively passed by AI platforms on click-through and pair it with AEO tooling — clean, current data without manual referrer tables that go stale.
